The Best Books According to Saadah

My Top Book Picks

The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck

This book is a refreshing take on self-help, encouraging readers to focus on what truly matters and let go of societal pressures. Mark Manson's candid and humorous writing style makes it an engaging read that challenges conventional wisdom about happiness and success.

Atomic Habits

This book provides a practical framework for building good habits and breaking bad ones. James Clear's evidence-based approach offers actionable strategies for making lasting changes in your life.

The Art of Letting God

In this book, author and spiritual teacher, Richard Rohr, explores the concept of surrendering to a higher power. He delves into the idea of letting go of control and trusting in the divine plan, offering insights on how to find peace and fulfillment through faith.
